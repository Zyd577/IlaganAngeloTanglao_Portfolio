BACK TO HOMEPAGE

Reflection

Creating this portfolio website with little prior knowledge of HTML and CSS was both a very challenging and rewarding journey. At first, I started watching YouTube videos about the concepts that I will need to start making my own website. After that, I experimented and started creating my website, but then the difficulty hit me. I had problems translating the ideas in my head into the lines of HTML and CSS that I needed to type. Along the way, as I tumbled through difficulties, I learned various concepts as I got stuck, and this became my motivation to keep going. This experience gave me a hard time, but it became a very rewarding experience, as my HTML and CSS proficiency increased greatly.
